BUCKiTDREAM

Everyone has a dream.

BUCKiTDREAM is a community of dreamers who live to make those dreams reality.

Create your dreams.

Build your own custom BUCKiTLIST and give your dreams a title, description, location, goal date and tags.

Document your dreams.

Use the “Add a new post” feature to upload photos about your dream journey, inspiring others on the way.

Achieve your dreams.

When you’ve completed a dream, tap “Mark as Complete” and keep track of the dreams you have accomplished.

Discover more dreams.

Browse through thousands of dream experiences from around the world and add them to your BUCKiTLIST to revisit later.

Privacy.

Some dreams are more private than others, so we've given you easy controls to keep a dream secret or visible to everyone.

Dream Bold, Dream Big!

More about BUCKiTDREAM
